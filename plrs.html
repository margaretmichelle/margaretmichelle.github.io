Overview

POLARIS is a fully autonomous sailboat designed completely by students to collect data in the Pacific Ocean. I lead the design and manufacturing of the hull and keel. We used Maxsurf to analyze and optimize hydrostatic and hydrodymanic properties, and SolidWorks and ANSYS to characterize her structure and select appropriate materials. In Fall 2024, we completed the manufacturing of her hull mainly involving large-scale carbon fibre layups over a wood core. Learn more about POLARIS here.
